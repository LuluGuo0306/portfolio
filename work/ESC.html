ESC Kids' Rain Gear

Overview

Background

Most of the kids like to play in the rain, even if not, there must be some chances that they need to walk or run in the rain. However, most of the rain gear on the market today is not designed for having heavy activities. A lot of children are unable to put rain gear on properly and when they take them off, it would end up making everything around them wet. Moreover, most rain gear does not provide complete protection from the rain. For example, it is dangerous for them to use the umbrella. Kids have a higher chance to get into an accident on a rainy day.
